1. What is UL2272 certificate aimed for ?

Since so many fire accident happened bcs of the low quality of hoverboard, USA -CPSC announced all the hoverboard sold on USA must follow the rules under UL2272 .

The aim of UL2272 is making sure the hoverboard is with second-time protection system to avoid the easy fire easy broken problems.
